Google Drive backup — optional

Your data is always saved to Firebase automatically on every device — Drive is just an optional extra backup mirrored to opptrack-backup.json in your Drive. Because Google only lets a site write to your Drive after you sign in, this is a per-device, one-time connect: click Connect Drive on a device and backups run quietly from then on. On devices where you don't connect, nothing pops up and your edits still save to Firebase as normal.

Security & sync
  • Your data is stored live in Firebase Firestore — every device that opens the site shows the same content, and your edits sync everywhere automatically.
  • Sign-in is handled by Firebase Authentication. Only the owner account can write; everyone else is read-only — enforced on the server, so it can't be bypassed from dev-tools.
  • To change your password: Firebase Console → Authentication → Users, or use the account's "reset password" email. Always Log out on shared devices.
